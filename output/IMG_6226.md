# Khuso’

- If you forget to do **sujud as-sahw** before **salam**:
  - And you remember, then do it after the salam.
  - If you left **masjid**, then you don't need to.

---

- If you do only one sujud in every rakah, then remember at the end (**tashahhud**):
  - **Malik**: make another sujud. That'll be his first rakah; build on that in tashahhud. Sujud will be after.

---

- **Imam Ahmad** has another opinion: to pray again, he is playing around, his heart was not in it.

---

- **Uthaymin**: If person leaves with imam, he doesn't need to do sujud behind imam.

---

- If person stands up to complete Salah & imam goes into sujud as-sahw after tashahhud, he continues as he has moved on & makes salah has finished anyway.

---

- **Khatib**: Whoever talks intentionally, his salah is invalid.

---

- **Abu Hurairah (AbuM)**: Before hijrah to Habasha, they spoke in salah. After, he spoke to **Muhammad** and he didn't reply. After, he said "in salah there is something that preoccupies".

---

- **Ahmad, Maliki, Shafi’i**: Talking forgetfully is overlooked.

---

- **Qudamah**: If he forgets he's in salah – it’s ok (**Maliki & Shafi’i**).

---

- If imam stands up for 5th rakah, you stay and complete.  
- If imam ends it early, stand up and complete salah.
