# Nullifiers of Salah

1. If an **ar'kaan** is left, salah is invalid.  
   If **shart** is left, salah is invalid.

2. If one can't, then they're excused in such situation.

3. **Ibn Abi Auf** — Ahmad told him to say tasbeeh, he just took his shahada.

4. If a person leaves a rukn, and can make it up, they do so and build it up from there.

5. If one forgets al-fatiha & remembers in next rakah, his salah starts in next rakah.

---

## Wājibāt

- Don't have to compensate for if behind an imam.
- If any are left **intentionally** then your salah is invalid.

1. Takbeerat al-intiqal
2. Tasbeeh in ruku
3. Tasbeeh in sujood
4. Tasbeeh - *samiallahuliman hamidah*
5. *Rabbana wa lakal-hamd*
6. *Rabbighfirlee*
7. Middle tashahhud
8. Salah on Muhmd

---

If one forgets **7 (Middle tashahhud)** intentionally, his salah is void.
