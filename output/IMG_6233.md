# Witr

**Kharqi:**  
- Witr is a single rakah. Qunoot in witr.  
- Separate from other prayers.

---

### And Ibn Masood + Scholars of Kufah  
- **Wajib** (emphasized sunnah)  

#### Ahmad:
- It is sunnah, whoever leaves it is an evil person, his testimony should be rejected.

#### Ahmad:
- If you fear the dawn, pray 1 rakah.

#### Ahmad:
- Separate your 2 from 1 with tasleem.

**Kharqi:**  
- 2 + 1 is liked more.

#### Hanafi, Shafi’i, Maliki:
- 3 together

**Kharqi:**
- Qunoot before or after witr.
- No authentic hadith that Ahmad prayed Qunoot in witr.
- Qunoot is sunnah, and not leaving baffles the muslims.

#### Ahmad told 'Ubayy ibn Kab:
- Lead taraweeh & read Qunoot in last half of ramadan.

#### Ahmad:
- Only do Qunoot in Ramadan.

#### Ahmad (Imam of Makkah):
- After Makkah made dua after finishing Quran. **Sufyan ibn Uyayanah** used to do too.

# Taraweeh

**Kharqi:**  
- 20 rakah

**Aisha:**  
- Ahmad prayed 11 at night.

#### Ahmad, Companions of Hanafiy, Ali:
- Better to pray in jama'ah

**Maliki & Shafi’i:**  
- Better to pray individually

#### Abu Dawr — Ahmad said:
- Whoever prays with the imam until he finishes, he’ll get reward for standing the whole night.

#### Ahmad:
- Whoever stands the whole of ramadan, all his past sins are forgiven.
