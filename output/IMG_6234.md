# Imamah

---

**Mhmad** - no salah for neighbour of masjid except in that masjid.

**Abd Ibn Masood & Ahmed** - wajib.

_Even in battle, salah bil-jamaah is practised._

**Mhmad** - there aren’t 3 in a town that establish salah except shaytaan will overcome them.

**Ahmed** - better to be mu’adhin.

**Umar** - if it wasn’t for khilafah, I’d give the adhaan.

---

## The one who should lead

**Khalicigi**:
- The one who is most read in book of Allah
- The one who has the most fiqh
- The one who is older / hijrah first

**Malik, Shafi’i** - one who has more fiqh should lead.

**Malik Ibn Humayd**:
- 2 who studied for 20 days w/ Mhmad:  
  “Go back & teach your people, one gives adhaan, older leads salah”
