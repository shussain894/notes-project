# Abdullah Ibn Umar

Abdullah Ibn Umar set out for jummah when a companion had passed away, he didn't attend jummah & busied himself with the janazah.

---

## Khiraji

1. **mustahabbaat**
2. ghusl - clean clothes
3. perfume

---

## Muwal

- Ghusl on jummah as it is wajib upon everyone who has reached puberty.

---

## Most Scholars

- Ghusl is not wajib.

---

## At-Kahfi

- Ahadith are weak, except for **Abu Sa'id al-Khudri**, but this one does not mention Friday.

---

- Setting on a journey **before jummah** is disliked, but you must set out before the zawwal.

---

## Khiraji

- If more than 3 miles, jummah is not wajib.
- They use the **hearing the adhaan** hadith – you can hear the adhaan from 3 miles *without noise pollution*.
