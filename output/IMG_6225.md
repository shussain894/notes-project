## Ahmad

> "If anyone of you forgets then let him do sujood as-sahw."

- *He gives for seven wajibat: adhan, takbeer, shoot.*

---

Scholars say when there’s an increase in salah, sujood is **after**.  
Decrease in salah, sujood is **before**.

> Like in hadith of **Dhul Yadayn** — when they made the rest of the salah up, they increased the salah.
