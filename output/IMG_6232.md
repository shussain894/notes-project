Abu Bakr prayed his qiyam after isha.  
Anas Ibn Malik said you can pray qiyam after maghrib.

### Hadith Qudsi
- "My slave does not draw closer to me except by doing what I made obligatory upon him.
- My slave continues to draw closer to me by doing Nawafil."

### Khusoof
- A person can pray nawafil sitting down.
- Person should sit in cross-legged position.
- In ruku/sujood he will sit in tashahhud position.

If a person is sick, sit, tired, lie. (*Imran bin Hussain*)

**Hadith said** "not able to"
