### Shaheed Neither Washed, Prayed Over, Nor Clothes Removed

**Ayy. Nawawi**:

1. **Shaheed of both worlds** - *mujahid*
2. **Shaheed of akhira not dunya** - *rites, plague, drown, crushed, stomach disease.*  
   *Treat these like normal deceased.*
3. **Shaheed of dunya not akhira** - buried as shaheed but *hypocrite*.

---

- **Martyr who is carried while holding breath of life is washed & prayed over**  
  - *Saad ibn Kd. Muadh* injured in field but not treated as shaheed (**Ahzab**).

- **If died on field then treated as one who died on field.**
