# Salat-ul-Khawf

## In Jihad

1. **When attacking**
2. **Being attacked**
   - _بيل اجتاح_
   - Abu Yusuf said prayer is only for Ahmad.

Ali, Hudhayfa prayed after Ahmad.

You can pray when running away from something in fear.

---

## If attacking

### Ahmad

- You can pray when attacking, even though some scholars say no. Uses hadith of Abdullah ibn Unays.
- Or when passing a border/boundary.

Ahmad: 6 ways to pray are narrated & are all allowed.

---

### 1. When enemy not in direction of Qibla

- Imam will pray 1st rakah with half of the camp.
- Group B will guard.
- Imam will stand & wait; group A will complete salah alone.
- Group B will join imam for the 2nd rakah.
- Imam will prolong tashahhud so group B can catch up. When imam goes in tashahhud position, group B will stand up & finish rakah & wait for group B.
- Imam can look back to see where groups are.
- 2 rakats becomes shortened due to travelling.  
  *(An-Nisa 102)*

---

#### Maghrib

- Imam will pray **2 rakah** with group A, they'll finish themselves.
- Imam will pray **1 rakah** with group B, they'll finish **2 themselves**.
