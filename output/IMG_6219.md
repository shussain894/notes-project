- *Khiraqi:* Whoever remembers a missed salah during salah, he should finish the salah then make up the missed one, then pray the one you were already praying again. 
  - Need to pray in order.

- *Muhammad:* Whoever slept or forgot a salah, let him pray it as soon as he remembers it.
  - (Applies to forgetting wudu’)

- If you intentionally missed salah, most scholars say make it up.
  - All 4 imams

- *Ibn Taymiyyah / Ibn Al-Qayyim:* He doesn’t make it up, true for everything Hajj is for 9th Arafah, can’t fast outside Ramadan, etc.

*Imam Shafi‘i*

- Making up the salah in order is not a condition.
  - E.g. you can pray Maghrib first, then ‘Isha, then Fajr.

- *Muhammad* prayed the prayers in order after he missed them in Battle of Khandaq.

- If you wake up with few mins of Fajr left but you missed ‘Isha - you pray Fajr first, then pray ‘Isha, then Fajr again.
  - *Imam Ahmad* said he doesn’t need to make up his Fajr.
  - Other scholars said he makes it up.

- No tabdee, just make up missed salah if it’s more than a day, no need to make the ones after it up.

- Even if it’s a year or years ago, Fuqaha said make it up but most likely not accepted.
  - difference between recent & older salawat

- *Muhammad* didn’t order the man who prayed badly to make up his salawat, even though he’d been praying like that all his life.
