# Salat ul-Kusoof

**Muhammad:**  
- Sun & moon are from signs of Allah, Allah uses them to put fear in the people.  
- When sun eclipsed when Ibrahim died.

---

**Eclipse is sign of Y.Q.** — ayah in Al-Qiyamah  
> "When the moon is eclipsed"

---

**Muhammad:**  
- When you see these signs, rush to salah & pray until it goes.  
    - "Why did you go forward & back?"  
    - "I saw heaven & tried grabbing the grapes, you would have eaten from them world, I saw hell. I never seen most of it was women, they are ungrateful."

---

- You can pray in jama'ah or alone.
- It's an emphasized sunnah.
- No adhaan or iqaamah.

---

**A'ishah:**  
- Read Al-Fatiha & a long surah (like Al-Baqarah),  
  Then do a long rukoo', come up & read Al-Fatiha & a long surah,  
  Then rukoo' & sujood, repeat for 2nd rak'ah.

---

**Muhammad:**  
- Pray & do dua & dhikr.  
  - (If you finish salah, dua & dhikr)

**ibn Abbas:**  
- "The alimam"

---

**Khatim:**  
- If eclipse is during prohibited time, do tasbeeh.
