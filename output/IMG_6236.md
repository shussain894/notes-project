# Prayer of the Traveller

Travelling 16 farsakh, 48 hashimi miles, he can shorten it **once he leaves his town**.  
- **Barcel**: man *takes* 4 men to get letter from Makkah to Madinah.  
- **Exposed** (jai)

---

### Ayuah

- An-nisa says shorten prayer if you fear disbelievers will harm you.
- Ma’lak ibn Hurayrith said to Umar: “We don’t fear anymore.”  
  **Umar asked Muhammad**: “It is sadaqa from Allah, so accept.”

When something isn’t legislated, you go to the custom at the time.

Distance of 48 miles is from outside of your town.

---

## Examples

- **Anas**: Muhammad shortened when he went 3 miles.
- **Ali**: Went just outside Kufa & shortened salah.

There is a view that you can only shorten if going for ibadah.

- **Abdullah ibn Mas’ud**: Only shortened for Hajj & Jihad.

---

### Opinions

- **Abu Hanifah**: Can shorten for anything.

Can not shorten if you intend for haram.

- **Khiraji**: If you didn’t intend to shorten salah & salah comes in, you cannot shorten it.

- **Abu Bakr (Hanbali) & Al-Mawqi (Twd. & Tayyaniyah)**: You don’t have to intend anything.

---

## Ijma

- Fajr & maghrib not shortened.

- **Khiraji**: He can shorten if he wants to, but he should do it.
- **Muhamad**: He can shorten, or pray full.
- **Hanabali & Tayyaniyah**: You must shorten it.

- **Ibn Abbas**: If you pray 4 while travelling, it is like praying 2 at home.
