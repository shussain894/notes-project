# Salat ul-Istisqa

*It is a command to seek rizq from Allah*

---

### Ruling:
- Only *jama'ah*, go out in state of humility.
- Wearing simple clothes; ***more likely to have dua accepted***

---

### Mihan
- A man may have wheat hair/clothes with holes, and he is pushed away when he knocks on houses, but if were to ask to Allah, He would accept it.  
  (*Known to at Hajj*)

---

### Mihan
- You are given victory (rizq) because of the dua of the weak among you.

---

## 2 rak'ats
- Set out for it, do Istighfar, ask to be free from the dhulm you've done to people.

- If you have a garment, you usually put on right, now to the left; likewise (jutma) (doesn't have to be done).

---

### Hanafi
- No salah, only dua.

---

*You can do it again if it doesn't rain.*

---

- Allah likes *ilhal*, when you ask again & again.
