## In hadith of Ibn Abbas
- Man lost his 2 garments. **Kafan** to be his 2 garments and didn't cover his head, or use perfume; he (the Prophet) will be raised up doing talbiyah.
- A man who dies in hajj, so perfume & camphor not used.
- **Kafan** doesn't need to be 3 sheets for men.

**Sa'id** - Asked **Muhammad** for his thawb, & it was his kafan.  
**Prophet** said: "In the khilafah, had wool clothing - 'That'll be my kafan as I thought in Badr with it.'"

**Aisha - Narrated**: Said bury him in 3 sheets (deceased).

- **Kafan** of woman is 5 (extra cloth for body & one for headscarf).
- **Muhammad**: Your best clothes are what you shroud in, those who use old.
