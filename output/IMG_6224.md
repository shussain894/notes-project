# Sujood as-Sahw

## Example

- Mi'mar was praying 4 rakah
- He stood up for 1st tashahhud
- He signalled, then finished sat up
- He finished salah
- And made up for it by e.g. 2 Sajda as-sahw

*(Imran ibn Hussain, Muslim)*

---

## Hanbali

### 2 cases where sujood as-sahw is **after tasleem**

1. **Where did Salam, some salah remaining**  
   He cuts his salah short, prayed 3, not 4.  
   He will make up the rak'ah or missed salah, then tasleem then sujood.

2. **Abu Hurairah:**  
   Mi'mar prayed 2 (forgetfully).  
   Dhu’l-Yadayn said did you forget or receive wahi?  
   He got reminder.  
   He prayed the rest, tasleem then sujood.

#### Hanafi

- E.g. lost rak'ah – tasleem once
- Sujood – sit up – sujood
- **Tashahhud after tasleem** – tashahhud again then tasleem

#### Imran ibn Hussain

- Hadith didn't mention tashahhud, tashahhud only mentioned in Tirmidhi.
- **Student of Ibn Sireen** (one of narrators) made mistake in narration re: tashahhud.

---

## 2.

- **Leading the prayer:** If imam has doubt, he does at-taharry (*weighs it up*).
- **Abdullah bin Masood:** If one of you has doubt in salah, let him look out for what's right then complete salah, then sujood as-sahw after at salaam.

---

### Sayyid Ibn Qudri

- If one of you has doubt, he forgets how much prayed, let him build on certainty then remove doubt then do sujood as-sahw **before** salaam.
