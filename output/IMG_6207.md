# Chapter of Adhaan

- If they knew virtue of adhaan + first row, they would compete — *hadith*
- Whoever hears your adhaan will testify, (jinn) — *hadith*
- Muadhin will have longest necks — *hadith*  
  (drowning on Day of Judgement)
 
- Dream of Abdullah Ibn Zayd — market seller said adhaan > bells - Adhaan of Bilal — Ahnaaf + Hanabilah prefer
- Abu Mahnura (Muadhin of Makkah), ashadu doubled each — First 4 said quietly — at-tarjee
  - preferred by Shafi'i + Malik
- Iqamah of Abu Mahzura chosen by Ahnaaf
- Assalatu khayrun min an-nawm — at-tathweeb


- Adhaan without wudu is makruh -da'if hadith from Abu Abdullah - comes from Mustahab to do dhikr in wudhu
- Abnaru ibn Yasir — to leave after adhaan has disobeyed Muhammad
  (wudu and toilets not part of masjid)

- Muadhin: Must be male, adult, and not doing it for ibadah.
- Can be a child.

- Good deeds of kids are written — *hadith of woman picking up child in Hajj*
- Adhaan + Iqamah — *Fardh Kifayah*
- Whoever follows muadhin sincerely, goes to jannah — *hadith*
- Repeating muadhin is sunnah - *Uthmaan sat on minbar + spoke to those in jama'ah whilst adhaan was given*

- If someone comes early, does ghusl + sits in front rows; for each step he gets, praying for one year, fasting for one year — *Abu Hurairah hadith*
- When 2 differ over qibla, pray individually.
