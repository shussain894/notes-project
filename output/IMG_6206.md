# Fiqh of Salah

Whoever misses asr – all his deeds gone – *hadith*
- Family & wealth gone

Time of isha is until middle of the night – *Abdullah bin Amr – hadith*

If a woman becomes pure at the time of praying, she should pray for maghrib & isha – *Ibn Abbas, Abdurrahman ibn Awf*
- Or if before maghrib, pray dhuhr & asr.

Fardh Salah is last at beginning time except for isha & dhuhr in heat – *hadith*

Praying salah at its beginning time is the best deed – *hadith*

Umar & co waited for Muhammad to pray salah.
"As you waited for salah, its as if you have been in salah, this would be the time of isha if it wasn’t hard for my nation"
1/3 of the night gone, some say half – *Umar – hadith*

Muhammad - "There will be some leaders that delay the salah"
- "What should we do?" - Abdullah bin Masood:  
  "Pray at its time and then in masjid behind them."

"Delay dhuhr in heat as it’s from jahannam." – *hadith*
- Cannot be applied to other salahs; jahannam is from unseen; heat of jahannam is only at dhuhr.
- For everyone, not just congregation.

"We never delayed jumu’ah" – *Umar – hadith*

The pure woman, new muslim & baligh child – *salah is wajib*
- If before sunset, they pray dhuhr & asr.

Allah sent messengers to tell right from wrong *hadith*

Salah is obligatory if:
Baliqh; age 15 – pubic hair – ejaculation – woman's period

The one who sleeps, child & insane > "The pen has been lifted from 3" – *hadith*
The one who sleeps through salah, prays when he wakes up
The one in coma is like the insane – some scholars: he will not wake up if you push him.

**Ammar ibn Yasir** – out for 3 days – he prayed all of his prayers.
