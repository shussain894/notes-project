### Ahmad

- Was asked about after Fajr where sun is red/yellow, he said he didn't like it, then mentioned Uqbah ibn Amir hadith.

---

### Taymiyyah

- You can pray janazah at these times;
  - We have 2 general statements, if one has been weakened, take the other one.

  (Ahmad prayed after Asr & Fajr)

---

### Hanafi, Maliki & Ahmad

- Do not pray at these 3 times:

1. After salat ul-Asr until sun has set.
2. After Fajr, after salat ul-Fajr, sunrise, sun and before the sun is at true **zawal**, not salat ul-Dhuhr after Fajr.
3. Zawal.
