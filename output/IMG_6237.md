### Khilaf: 
- When dhuhr starts, pray only dhuhr, pray each salah in its time.
- If he wants, he can delay or combine.

---

#### An-Nawawi in Majmoo’ & Al Ghazali
- Said consensus to not combine.

---

- Bringing one salah forward — *Jamu’ at-taqdeem* — *Some scholars say can’t do this*
- Pushing one salah back:

---

#### Anas:
- Ahmad prayed dhuhr + asr at dhuhr.
- If he started & dhuhr not come in, he prayed dhuhr + asr at asr.

---

- Can only combine; traveling, sick, fear, rain.

---

#### Tayseeriyyat:
- Whole point is to remove hardness.

---

#### Ibn Qudamah in Al-Mughni:
- If you go masjid under shelter in rain, or live in masjid, you can’t combine.

---

### Khilaf:
- If a person forgets to pray at home then travelled, then remembered.
- If a person forgets to pray, when travelling then remembered at home:
  - If he left, he does it at home.

---

- Sunnah to pray behind imam as he prays.
- If a traveller comes late, he still makes it up. — *Abdullah ibn Umar in Sahih Muslim*

---

#### Muwallad
- A visitor shouldn't lead them in salah.

---

## How long can a person shorten for?

#### Ahmad:
- 21 salawaat.

---

#### Abdullah Ibn Abbas:
- If go for 18 days, will shorten as *Muhammad* alone that.

---

#### Anas & *Muhammad*:
- Went Makkah for 10 days & shortened.

---

#### Ahmad:
- In the above 2, he was in one place for 21 salawaat, then moved.

---

- If you didn't intend to go for more than 21 salawaat, don't shorten.

---

- If someone is unsure when they'll leave, may shorten, even forever.  
  *Same Sahabah shortened for 6 months.*

---

#### Tayseeriyyat:
- Shorten for as long as you’re gone.

#### Aisha:
- Shorten until you put down provisions + rucksack.
