# Al-Janazah

1. **Takbeeratul Ihram**  
   *Bukhari*

2. **Al-Fatihah** (*Ibn Abbas*) (**Ahmad**)  
   - I read loud so you can know the surah

3. **Second Takbeer** – Salat upon Muhammad

4. **Third Takbeer – duaa**  
   - **Awf b. Malik** – specific janazah duaa  
   - **Imam Ahmad** – can make any duaa from oneself

5. **Fourth Takbeer** – some scholars say you can make any duaa here

6. **One Tasleem** (*6 companions + Imam Ahmad*)  
   - **Raise hands with every takbeer**

---

- Can consider duaa as pillar.
- Don’t have to make up missed takbeerat.

---

- Can pray near body if it’s still there or over grave.  
  **Ahmad** – can pray if body not present (**Wejeashi**)
- Deceased entered feet first. Treat them with ihsan.
- Woman’s grave covered with a sheet (**mustahabb**)
  - *Mahram lays her; if more than one, old women, more than old men.*
- Shroud not cut, knots untied.
- Scholars dislike anything touched by fire being placed in grave.

---

- If imam does 5 takbeers do 5. **Ahmad, Ali** done this.
- Imam should stand at chest (shoulder) of male, and middle of female (*Like Muhammad*).
