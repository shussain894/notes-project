### Qudsi:

### Muhammad
Whoever visits the sick in the morning, 70,000 angels will send salat on the sick until evening, & evening till morning.

---

### Always speak good or with hope to the sick.

---

### Muhammad
Urge your dying to say la ilaha ill-Allah.

---

### Say nice things to the dying as they did to Amr bin Aas.

---

### Muhammad
A person should have husn al-dhan of Allah when they're dying.

It is not that a person has these two (hope & husn al-dhan) that Allah will give it to them.

- ... jannah

---

### Khumasi
Once certain he is dead, face him towards the Qibla, his eyes are closed.

**Muhammad**: Closed Abu Salamah's eyes, his eyes follow the rooh.

Close the jaw, place something heavy on stomach, no bloating.

**Muhammad**: Do not wail or damage yourselves.

---

## Ghusl — Fardh kifayah

- Remove clothes & cover his awrah w/ sheet.
- Put something on his hand, towel or cloth, clean area if janazah.
- Press on the stomach.

**Muhammad**: Start w/ right hand side & start w/ wudhu.

- Rub front of clothes leg on hair & wash w/ water, make sure no water goes in.

**Muhammad**: Breaking bone of dead is same as breaking bone of living.

- In final wash, use camphor mixed w/ dried leaves.

**Muhammad**: Wash 3, 5, or 7 times.  
**A'ishah**: 7 is limit.
