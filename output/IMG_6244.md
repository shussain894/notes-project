# Ali
- Told someone to pray 4 rakaat with elderly, without khutbah.

---

1. **Ahmad** – Pray 4 if you missed eid salah.  
   *Not qaza but substitute.*

2. **Cannot make up eid salah** (*fardh kifayah*).

3. **Ibn Sireen** – You can make qaza of eid, 2 rakaat.

---

## Khiraji
- Takbir starts from day of arafah, fajr time – *Umar, Ali*
- Until maghrib of last day of tashreeq.  
  *Also: Masood, ibn Abbas*

**Khiraji** – Takbir after each salah in jama'ah.

**Abdullah ibn Ahmad** – Can do takbir after each fardh salah, even by yourself.

---

## Takbirat

1. After each salah in jama'ah
2. At any time during the day – *takbirat mutlaq*
